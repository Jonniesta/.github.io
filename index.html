About Me

I’m currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Psychology after completing my associate’s in Human Services, with a deep commitment to understanding people and the experiences that shape them. My purpose is rooted in helping the next generation feel seen, heard, and supported in ways that many of us didn’t always have growing up. I want to become a therapist who creates a safe space where people can be honest about what they’re carrying, and where healing feels possible and real. Everything I do is guided by empathy, patience, and the belief that small moments of understanding can change someone’s entire direction in life.
