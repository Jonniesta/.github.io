About Me

Helping People Feel Seen, Heard, and Understood

I’m currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Psychology after completing my associate’s in Human Services, with a deep commitment to understanding people and the experiences that shape them.

My purpose is rooted in helping the next generation feel supported in ways many of us didn’t always have growing up. I hope to become a therapist who creates safe spaces where people can be honest about what they’re carrying and where healing feels possible and real.

Everything I do is guided by empathy, patience, creativity, and the belief that moments of understanding can change someone’s entire direction in life.
